About Me

Pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Interactive Arts & Technology at Simon Fraser University, I specialize in Design and Interactive Systems. Whether it's coding lines or crafting cuisines, each endeavour contributes a unique contour to my multifaceted profile – one where creativity meets technology.
